# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our digital products.

## Theme Overview

Our current design theme emphasizes:
- **Color Mode**: Dark
- **Seed Color**: `#8b5cf6`
- **Color Variant**: Fidelity
- **Roundedness**: Subtle (1)
- **Spacing**: Normal (2)

## Typography

We leverage a distinct set of fonts to establish a clear hierarchy and enhance readability.
- **Headlines**: Space Grotesk
- **Body Text**: Inter
- **Labels**: Inter

## Color Palette

Our brand colors are chosen for consistency and impact, defining the visual identity across all interfaces.
- **Primary Color**: `#8b5cf6` (A vibrant, engaging purple for primary actions and branding)
- **Secondary Color**: `#f59e0b` (A warm, inviting orange for supporting elements)
- **Tertiary Color**: `#ef4444` (A bold red for accents and highlights)
- **Neutral Color**: `#0f172a` (A deep, rich dark blue for backgrounds and foundational elements)
